Who I Am

I'm a tech enthusiast that enjoys learning all of the newest and latest technologies. I've loved learning about tech since I was 4 years old when my family got our first computer. I was a gamer at an early age and learned how to install mods for minecraft and set up servers on my own. I love to tinker around with all sorts of tech gadgets and learn how to troubleshoot new problems I encounter.

I hold a B.S. in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance from Western Governors University, and have earned several industry recognized certifications such as CompTIA Pentest+ and CompTIA CySA+. I am currently continuing my education and skills by building home labs, personal projects, and working towards earning more relavant certifications.

My goals are to learn all that I can about the latest tech and how to prevent the latest forms of cyber attacks, and to help others learn and prevent them as well. I hope to be an incredible asset to a team or companies to help fortify against these latest attacks.
